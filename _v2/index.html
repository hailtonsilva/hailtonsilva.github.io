Working Process

My working process is very simple and oriented to best practices, followed by three understandable steps:

Plan

Time to organize things, listen and recognize trends and ideas and then analyze their strengths and weakness, to then start the wireframe.

Design

With the skeleton defined, it's the time to think more graphically (colors, images, typography, forms, buttons, etc).

Code

With everything defined, it's the time to transform the pixel in clean and lightweight code, ready for any device (desktop, tablet or mobile).
